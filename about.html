About Us

Founded in 2001 when the singers of Raffles Chorale who won the Best Choir of the Year in 1999 came together again to sing under the baton of award-winning conductor Toh. The inaugural concert was an expected full house at the 900-seater Victoria Concert Hall. Since then, the choir has continued to be the platform of choral excellence for choral conductors, teachers and singers, who have graduated from school choirs that had won top prizes in Europe and Singapore.

2006 saw Raffles Singers win a perfect-score Gold diploma and Category Champion in the 34th International Festival of Songs in Olomouc. In 2007, it won another Gold at the 17th International Festival of Advent and Christmas Music in Prague. In 2008, the choir was invited to perform at the inaugural International Garden Festival of Singapore.

In June 2015, Raffles Singers flew Singapore’s flag high in the 3rd international Copernicus Choir Festival and Competition in Torun, Poland, when it bagged a Gold Diploma, Category Winner, and above all the Grand Prix. The week prior, the Singers also sang during a mass at the Holy Cross Church, where Chopin’s heart was kept, and received a standing ovation after giving a concert there.

From annual Vocal Exploits concert to Christmas caroling, the Singers have continued to engage Singapore choral scene with its programming and fund-raising for community and charity. The Singers also collaborates with orchestra in presenting bigger choral works. The Singers is committed to programming substantial period music in Singapore as it explores the modern repertoire.
