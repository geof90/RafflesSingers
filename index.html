Welcome

to the home page of a premier mixed choir based in the beautiful island city-state in Southeast Asia. Continuing the passion of the award-winning Raffles Chorale and Voices, singers from all professions here make a unique blend with their vast musical experiences and intellect. Not forsaking the period music that are rarely performed well in Asia, the Singers embrace new music and pushes the boundaries.
